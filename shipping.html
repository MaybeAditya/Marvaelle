Shipping Policy

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

Shipping Rates & Delivery

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

  • Domestic Shipping (India): 5-7 business days.
  • International Shipping: 10-15 business days.

You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email containing your tracking number(s) once your order has shipped.

Customs & Duties (International)

Édition de Marvaelle is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).
