Privacy Policy

At Édition de Marvaelle, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect and use your information.

What We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Identity Data: Name, title.
  • Contact Data: Email address, billing address, delivery address, phone number.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments and the products you purchase.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and other technology on the devices you use to access this website.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Process and deliver your order.
  • Manage payments and prevent fraud.
  • Communicate with you about your order or account.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We do not store your full credit card details.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Please contact us at business.uut@gmail.com to do so.
