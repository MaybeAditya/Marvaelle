Refund & Cancellation Policy

Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order at any time before it has been shipped. To cancel your order, please contact us at business.uut@gmail.com with your order number.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns on unworn, unused items with all original tags attached within 14 days of delivery.

To initiate a return, please email us at [Your Support Email] with your order number and the reason for the return. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

Please note: Sale items are non-refundable.
